Welcome!

The Business Entity Modeler is part of the AAF Data product from Deceptively Simple Technologies. The modeler is provided to help AafCoreModelers to create new and to clone existing and to publish EntityTypeDefinitions and EntityTypeAttributes, to associate EntityTypeAttributes with and dissociate them from EntityTypeDefinitions, and to publish these, creating corresponding new database schemas, tables, constraints, indexes, etc
